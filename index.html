NanoDrop.io

A brand-new Nano faucet: safe, open source, replicable, auditable and embeddable.
Allows new users to receive a small micro-fraction of Nano to test the cryptocurrency.

The mechanism is similar to solving a Google recaptcha and can be integrated into websites.

Nano Rockets

Launched

Nano Block Viewer - "To the Moon!"

Each rocket represents a confirmed Nano tx and it shows the natricon of the sender's account.
Its inclination is defined by the hash of the block.
Its speed is defined by the difficulty multiplier of the block's PoW.
