About me

Hello! I'm Kaique Nunes, also known as Anarkrypto, a Brazilian Senior Full Stack Web Developer with over 8 years of professional experience crafting robust and scalable web solutions.

As a self-taught engineer, I began my journey in programming and honed my expertise in web development since 2017. My deep dive into Web3 has enabled me to deliver innovative and high-quality solutions.

I'm deeply passionate about Web3, Blockchain, and P2P networks. Over the years, I've successfully integrated these decentralized technologies into both personal and enterprise-grade projects, driving innovation and efficiency.

As a versatile full stack developer, I specialize in building end-to-end web services using modern tools and frameworks, with a strong emphasis on TypeScript for maintainable code. From architecting performant back-end systems to designing intuitive front-end interfaces, I bring clarity, precision, and confidence to every project.
